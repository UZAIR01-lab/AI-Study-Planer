AI Smart Study Planner

BTech CSE · Artificial Intelligence · 20 March 2026

Developed by Aatif Shaikh & Uzair Pathan. An AI-based system that analyzes exam dates, subject difficulty, and available study hours to automatically generate an optimized daily study timetable.

Objectives

  • Build an AI-based study planner
  • Prioritize subjects by exam dates
  • Allocate study hours efficiently
  • Generate daily timetable automatically

Tools Used

  • Python – Programming language
  • Streamlit – Web interface
  • NumPy – Math calculations
  • Cost per user: ₹0 (all free)

Data Used

  • Subjects & exam dates (survey)
  • Daily study hours available
  • Subject difficulty level
  • Numerical + categorical data

Future Improvements

  • Mobile app version
  • Reminder notifications
  • Calendar integration
  • Agentic auto-adjustment
AI Logic / Priority Formula
Priority Score = (1 / days_left) × difficulty_weight

Example:
Calculus (2 days, Hard) → Score = 0.50 × 3 = 1.50 — HIGH
DLD (5 days, Medium) → Score = 0.20 × 2 = 0.40 — MEDIUM
Chemistry(10 days, Easy) → Score = 0.10 × 1 = 0.10 — LOW
